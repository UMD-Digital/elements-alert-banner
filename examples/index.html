Alert 1

This alert has no attrbutes applied to it, except for a unique storage-key, Styling is handled almost completely by the lightDOM.

Alert 2

A second alert with a “primary” alert type. The styles are more strictly controlled when a type is applied to the element. This alert also has padding="20px", and max-width="1280px" attributes.

Alert 3

A third alert with a “warning” alert type. This alert also has padding="20px", and max-width="1024px" attributes.

Alert 4

A fourth alert with a custom alert type, went with blue. This alert also has padding="20px", and max-width="1024px" attributes and cannot be dismissed/removed because the "Close" [X] button is not rendered when the dismissable="false" attribute is applied.

Alert 5

A fifth alert with a “danger” alert type. This alert has padding="20px", and max-width="768px" attributes.
